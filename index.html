A bit of background

What I'm working on and where I come from.

I'm in my final year of a BSc in Information Technology at the University of Lusaka, with coursework spanning IT security, systems administration, and enterprise networking from configuring Active Directory and DNS/DHCP on Windows Server to designing multi-site VLAN and VPN topologies in Cisco Packet Tracer.

Alongside my studies, I run a small freelance web development practice, building Bootstrap-based websites for local businesses in Zambia. My main personal project, CropIQ, is a Flutter mobile app that gives Zambian smallholder farmers AI-powered crop recommendations based on rainfall, soil, and climate data.

I'm currently part of the B360 Career Bridge Program, a structured internship pipeline connecting students like me with companies in Europe and I'm always open to internship, junior sysadmin, or freelance web opportunities.
